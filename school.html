Smart Attendance System

The Smart Attendance System utilizes RFID technology for seamless student check-ins and real-time attendance notifications.

  • Automated tracking of student attendance
  • RFID tags embedded in student ID cards
  • Notifications sent to parents for real-time updates

How It Works

When a student enters the school, the RFID reader scans their ID card. The presence is logged automatically, and parents receive a notification on their mobile devices.

  • Quick and efficient check-in process
  • Reduced absenteeism through immediate notifications
  • Data stored securely for record-keeping

Benefits of the Smart Attendance System

Feature Smart Attendance System
Ease of Use Simple check-in process for students; one scan and done!
Real-time Communication Instant notifications sent to parents regarding attendance.
